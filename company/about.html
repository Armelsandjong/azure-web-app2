Our Story

Founded in 2017, AFRIQUEEUROPECONNEXIONVMETC (AEC) emerged from a vision to create meaningful connections between African and European businesses through digital transformation and innovative technology solutions.

What started as a mission to bridge the digital divide has evolved into a comprehensive digital transformation partner serving businesses across both continents. We combine deep technical expertise with cultural understanding to deliver solutions that truly resonate with diverse markets.

Today, with offices in Douala, Cameroon and Kraków, Nigeria, we're proud to have helped dozens of businesses leverage technology to achieve their goals, expand their reach, and drive sustainable growth.
