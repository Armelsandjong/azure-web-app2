Frequently Asked Questions

What accounting software do you recommend?

It depends on your size and sector. QuickBooks or Xero for SMEs, SAP or D365 for larger enterprises. We help you select and implement the right system — and can migrate your existing data.

How often should financial statements be prepared?

Monthly for operational monitoring, quarterly for board reporting, and annually for statutory compliance. Frequency depends on your business complexity and stakeholder requirements.

Can you handle multi-currency accounting?

Yes — we specialise in multi-currency accounting, FX management, and consolidated reporting for businesses operating across Africa, Europe, and beyond, including EUR, PLN, XAF, and NGN.

Do you cover OHADA accounting standards?

Absolutely. We have deep expertise in SYSCOHADA (the OHADA accounting framework), applicable across all 17 member states in West and Central Africa, and can align it with IFRS for group reporting.

Do you offer fractional CFO services?

Yes. Our fractional CFO service provides strategic financial leadership part-time or per-project — giving you executive-level financial expertise without the cost of a full-time hire.

How do you ensure data security?

We use encrypted cloud-based systems, secure file transfer, and strict access controls. All staff sign NDAs and we comply with GDPR and applicable data protection standards in our jurisdictions.
