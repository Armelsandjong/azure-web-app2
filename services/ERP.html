Unified Cross-Border Operations

In today's global economy, a business in Cameroon or Nigeria must speak the same digital language as its partners in Poland or France. At AFRIQUEEUROPECONNEXIONVMETC, we don't just "install software"—we build digital bridges.

The Right Platform for Your Needs

Microsoft Dynamics 365

The Advantage: Unmatched ecosystem synergy. D365 integrates natively with Power BI for analytics and Microsoft Copilot for AI-driven trend analysis.

AEC Benefit: We specialize in using D365 to streamline cross-border finance and supply chains between European HQs and African branches.

Odoo ERP

The Advantage: Modular, flexible, and cost-effective. Odoo allows businesses to start with what they need (CRM/Accounting) and scale infinitely.

AEC Benefit: We provide "Micro-SaaS Scripts"—localized scripts that plug into Odoo to handle regional B2B operations and unique tax structures instantly.

SAP S/4HANA

The Advantage: The global gold standard for operational rigor. Best suited for high-complexity manufacturing and global enterprises.

AEC Benefit: We apply our deep Financial Business Analysis to SAP’s high-power system, optimizing complex internal processes that standard consultants miss.

Oracle NetSuite

The Advantage: The world’s #1 cloud-native ERP. Oracle offers superior financial management for companies that require strict audit trails and transparency.

AEC Benefit: We integrate Oracle with your "FinTech & ERP Marketplace," ensuring your data flows securely through Azure Cloud architecture.
